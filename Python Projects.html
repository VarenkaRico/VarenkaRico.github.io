PyWeather

Summary

Visualizations

This project's main objective was the use of APIs, in this case, the OpenWeatherMap.org Through the Python Script, it is possible to visualize the weather of 500+ cities across the world of varying distance from the equator.

This script was designed to obtain the following graphs:

  • Temperature (F) vs. Latitude
  • Humidity (%) vs. Latitude
  • Cloudiness (%) vs. Latitude
  • Wind Speed (mph) vs. Latitude
