Temperature Vs Latitude

Visualizations

As expected, the closer to the Equator (Latitude 0), the hotter it gets. But it seems it is not the only factor that should be considered. Notice that the Northern Hemisphere (Positive Latitudes) has a higher temperature than the Southern Hemisphere (Negative Latitudes). This is most likely due to the Earth's tilt and the reason behind having different seasons in the Northern and Southern Hemisphere at the same time.
